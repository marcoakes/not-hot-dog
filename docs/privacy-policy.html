Information We Collect

We collect absolutely nothing.

Not Hot Dog is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not collect usage analytics
  • Does not use tracking or advertising
  • Does not require an account or registration
  • Does not access your contacts, location, or other data

Camera Usage

The app requires camera access to function. Here's how we handle it:

  • Purpose: The camera is used solely to capture images for hot dog classification
  • Processing: All image analysis happens entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework
  • Storage: Images are not saved to your photo library unless you explicitly choose to do so
  • Transmission: Images are never uploaded to any server or cloud service

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app is completely standalone.

Data Storage

Since we don't collect any data, there is nothing to store. The app does not maintain any databases, user profiles, or historical records of your usage.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.
